Header type primitives.

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Sat · 27 June</Eyebrow>
<PageTitle>Today</PageTitle>
```

`Eyebrow` is the monospace, uppercase, tracked-out label used for the date line above a page title and for date-group headers in Upcoming (e.g. `TOMORROW · SUN 28`). `PageTitle` is the 28/300 view title. Keep titles to one or two words.
